# Skillboxing

A persistent learning system for knowledge work — contracts, startup operations, and business projects.

## Brain vault

The `brain/` directory is your persistent knowledge base. It survives across sessions.

### Before acting

- Read `brain/index.md` to see what knowledge exists
- Read the relevant brain files before starting any task
- Apply principles and past learnings to current work

### After acting

- Document mistakes, corrections, and notable learnings
- One topic per file, directories with `[[wikilink]]` indexes
- Delete outdated notes; merge overlapping ones
- Never inline large content — link to separate files

### Structure rules

- Filenames: lowercase, hyphenated, `.md`
- Keep notes under ~50 lines
- Bullets over prose, no YAML frontmatter in brain files
- Wikilinks: `[[section/file-name]]`

## Domains

This system covers two primary domains:

1. **Contracts** — review, drafting, negotiation, clause analysis
2. **Startup operations** — formation, hiring, project management, governance

## Working with documents

- Always read the full document before modifying any part
- Track changes and concessions explicitly
- Flag ambiguity and risk even when not asked
- Verify facts, dates, names, and figures against source documents
- Prefer precision over brevity in legal and business contexts
